Once upon a sunset red,
  Tomato Red heard someone said,
    “To stir her heart, her fire, her head—
      A rose of red that said, ‘To bed.’”

For Passion Red did he outspread
  This present duly bred for red.
    Alarmed and armed was Passion Red;
      She stabbed the fruit! Left Red for dead!

Such is the dread of Passion Red
  Whose cherry many dreamed to wed.
    Pray laud the dead, disheart'nd, and Red,
      For love had they not fled but bled.
